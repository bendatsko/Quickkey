about
quickkey is a minimalistic typing test with word sets composed of an assortment of english's 1000 most common words.
results
wpm - the number of words typed per minute (accounts for errors).
acc - typing accuracy, displayed as a percentage of typed characters over the total number of characters in a specific word set.
wpm/time graph
a graph displaying fluctuations in raw wpm (wpm without regards to accuracy) throughout a typing test.
